Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at School of Vehicle and Mobility, Tsinghua University under the guidance of Prof. Shengbo Eben Li. I also receive co-supervision from Prof. Chang Liu and Prof. Jianyu Chen. In January 2023, I started a visiting research position at the Department of Computer Science, in the Univeristy of Manchester , mentored by Prof. Wei Pan.

My research interests are deeply rooted in optimal filtering and control, probabilistic inference, and their practical applications to autonomous vehicles.
