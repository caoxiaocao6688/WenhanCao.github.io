Biography

I am a Ph.D. candidate at School of Vehicle and Mobility, Tsinghua University under the guidance of Prof. Shengbo Eben Li. I also receive co-supervision from Prof. Chang Liu and Prof. Jianyu Chen.

My research interests are optimal control and estimation using Bayesian machine learning, with applications to autonomous vehicles and robots.
